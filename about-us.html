About Us

Founded in 1984, Global Softnet is the pioneer of organized recruitment services in India. Over the years, we have acted as preferred recruitment partners to multinationals and leading Indian businesses to emerge as the leading recruitment brand nationally. This combined with our role as trusted consultants for Indian professionals translates into our core capability - Building Careers, Building Organizations.


We proactively build and maintain enduring relationships with high quality professionals through the lifecycle of their careers. We carefully nurture these relationships with the support of our world-class technology platform and provide personalized attention to each applicant, advising them on their career paths and helping them navigate through the recruitment process, enriching the overall relationship.


Global Softnet is proud of its long standing relationships in the industry and the fact that 80% of its revenue comes from its existing clients, which is a testimonial of happy clients.
